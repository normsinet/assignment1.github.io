I was born in Siem Reap province. I live there for a long time but when I finish my high school I leave my home town change to live Phnom Penh capital for working and studing.

Siem Reap is a captivating province in northwestern Cambodia, renowned for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant atmosphere. As a hometown, Siem Reap offers a blend of historical charm and modern convenience.

The town is perhaps best known as the gateway to the Angkor Archaeological Park, home to the iconic Angkor Wat temple complex. This UNESCO World Heritage site draws visitors from around the globe, intrigued by its ancient temples, intricate carvings, and historical significance. Living in Siem Reap means having these awe-inspiring ruins as a backdrop to daily life.

Cultural traditions are deeply embedded in daily life. Festivals like Khmer New Year and the Water Festival are celebrated with enthusiasm, featuring traditional dances, music, and vibrant parades.
